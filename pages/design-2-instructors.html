5 steps to book an instructor

How booking works

1Step

Choose your instructor

Browse profiles, experience, reviews and languages. Select the instructor that fits you best and click Book now.

2Step

Submit your lesson details

Enter your preferred dates, total lesson hours, number of participants, skill level and any special requests. Leave your contact information and send the request.

Form
3Step

Request received

You’ll see a confirmation screen with your booking reference number.
There is no need to stay on the page — we take it from here.

4Step

Schedule coordination

Our booking manager contacts you via WhatsApp to confirm the instructor’s availability and finalize the details.

5Step

Secure payment and confirmation

You receive an email with your booking details and a secure payment link on our website. After completing the prepayment through the payment system, your lessons are officially reserved.

Once the payment is processed, you receive a final confirmation email along with the instructor’s direct contact details, so you can coordinate the meeting and final arrangements.
