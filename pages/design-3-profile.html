About

I’m a certified ski instructor with over eight years of teaching experience in Gudauri. I work with adults and teenagers who want to feel confident and in control on the mountain, whether it’s their first day on skis or they are refining existing skills.

My lessons are structured and goal-oriented. I focus on proper technique from the beginning, clear explanations and practical exercises that bring steady improvement. Safety, comfort and visible progress are always the priority.

Each lesson is adapted to your level, pace and personal goals. Whether you want to learn the basics, improve carving technique or gain more confidence on red slopes, we build the lesson around what you need most.
